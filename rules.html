Rules & Instructions

Mobile-friendly page — no zooming/panning needed.

Instructions to have a “verified” speedrun:

  1. Enable the speedrun timer before you jump into the map you will speedrun. This can be done by navigating to the homepage of the Ball Guys app —> Settings (Gear Icon in top right) —> Click “Speedrun clock”.
  2. 1a. When you start the map, there should show a red timer that starts running immediately. If you don't see it, try restarting your app.
  3. 2. Important: Please start screen recording from before you fire your first shot!
    Clarification notes: It is generally best practice to respawn after setting up the screen recorder, because our leaderboard will use the official clock time no matter what — NOT from when you start shooting. If you fall in lava and auto respawn after this, there’s no need to begin the hassle of a whole new screen recording, just keep attempting and crop the video afterwards from before the clock was at 00:00 :)
  4. 3. IMPORTANT: Once you have completed a run, DO NOT respawn, and DO NOT end the recording yet — go to the menu, and press “spectate”. This will allow the moderators to see the green timer with your precise and official time :)
    Note: Runs that neglect to do this final step can still be verified as we will estimate the time — but bare in mind we round up to the nearest second in such cases which may cause your run to be less competitive — so please don't forget this final step :)

Rules to have a “verified” glitchless run:

  • Runs must be fully recorded, uncut, unedited, and run from before the start until after the finish.
  • Runs must not use any mods or glitches, yes, even the flying glitch (Sorry guys XD).
  • Pauses of any kind, anything that freezes the clock, or your ball, such as using the control center etc. are not allowed and will cause a run to be rejected.

Verified recordings will be kept in an archive by myself and linked publicly on the leaderboard for future inspection and transparency. The videos are mainly linked for education and entertainment, but anyone browsing the leaderboard will be able to replay your recording and report foul play/editting should they suspect it, which will cause us to re-review them.

If anyone would like to be part of that moderation team to help verify runs, let me (Abe) know through discord! Here is a link to the Ball Guys Discord Server. Once there, Search “Abe” or “icarus1892” in the member search (Additionally, a direct link to the discord can be found at the top right corner of the main menu in ball guys under the gear icon.)

One caveat — Screenshots will be considered “unverified” by default. Not because I don't trust anybody in specific, just because I want standard to be applied consistently to all runs :)

For the time being, submissions will be filed into 3 categories:

  1. Unverified: Simple screenshots with no recording, or rejected videos that didn't follow the rules and instructions above
  2. Pending Verification: Recordings that were submitted but not yet reviewed in detail by moderation team
  3. Verified: Recording has been reviewed and approved by the moderation team

To be clear, ALL submitted runs (at least a screenshot of your time) will be tracked on the leaderboard, just marked with their corresponding categorization! :)
